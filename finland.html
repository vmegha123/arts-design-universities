Finland – Arts & Design Universities

Aalto University

Global Ranking: Top 100 in Art & Design globally

Programs: BA & MA in Design, Visual Communication, Fashion, Architecture

Entry Requirements: Portfolio, English proficiency (IELTS 6.5+), academic transcripts

Application Deadline: January (varies yearly)

Portfolio: 8–12 creative works, concept development required

Tampere University

Global Ranking: Medium competition, well recognized in Europe

Programs: Interactive Media, Visual Arts, Digital Design

Entry Requirements: Portfolio + entrance task

Application Deadline: January

Portfolio: Creative projects showing process, sketches, final outcomes
